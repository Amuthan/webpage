    AE 337/AE 731 - Multiscale Modeling of Materials

    Fall 2018

    This is a new graduate level elective dealing with various aspects of modeling the mechanical behavior of crystalline materials across various length and time scales, from atomistic to the continuum. Topics covered include basic ideas in Quantum, Classical, Statistical and Continuum Mechanics, along with relevant numerical techniques. Assignments for this course will be uploaded on the IITB Moodle.
